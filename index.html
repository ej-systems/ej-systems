About

A small company building focused tools.

EJ Systems LLC is an Ohio-based technology company founded in 2025. We're a lean operation — focused on building a small number of products well rather than spreading thin across a dozen ideas.

Our background is in homelab infrastructure, software development, and systems automation. We're makers at heart, which means we tend to build things we wish already existed.

Ohio Rate Watch started because of a personal experience getting burned by an energy supplier contract with buried fees. We figured if it happened to us, it was happening to a lot of people who had even fewer tools to protect themselves.
